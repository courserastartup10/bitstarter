Proof reader

Crowed source your proof reading.

Proof reader is designed to get feedback on your novel or draft.
You can for example publish the first chapter and some key words for the rest of the book and let others come with comment, suggestions and tips on how they feel the continuation of the book should be.
Or why not publish the whole novel as a draft to get help with the spelling and grammars and to recive feedback if there is a hole in the plot.
